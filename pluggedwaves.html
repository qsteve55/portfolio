PLUGGED WAVES

Plugged Waves is a web application, that is made for independent artists to receive donations from fan supporters. This can establish a more intimate relationship between artist and fans. Fans are also rewarded by, receiving award points. And with reward points, fans can purchase items at a discounted price.

PROBLEM

Sometimes it's hard for new artists to gather support behind them. A new independent artist is looking for a place that can help reach a bigger fan base as well as gather financial support for upcoming projects and tours.

SOLUTION

Plugged Waves can offer support for an independent artist that what's to bridge the gap between artist and supporter. Plugged Waves is the perfect crowd-funder for a new artist looking for backing on projects.

RESEARCH

While working on this project I found out that 98% of the polled participants listen to music on their mobile device. And 74% of the participants enjoy finding a new independent artist. As well as 68% of participants would not mind financial supporting an independent artist.

COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

Looking at top streaming services as a reference point on how the UI/UX will operate smoothly. Gave me perspective on how I was going to design the web layout. I was trying to see what do these companies offer independent artists and how the people who support them will be compensated. 

WIREFRAME/TESTING

With this wireframe, I got a general layout of the sites' homepage. I tested out to see if it still fits the mold for a modern design. As I received feedback for the design, I made changes to certain elements to make it more user-friendly.

LOGO

I was testing out different logos to see which one as a long-lasting appeal, and still as an original feel. During this process, I experimented with different geometric shapes. 

FINAL DESIGN/TESTING

I took the feedback that I have received from the previous testing and improved upon it. By changing the typography, it gave the design a more sleek and modern feel to it. Also by making sure the hero for the home page was standing out as well.

VIEW PROTOTPYE

CONCLUSION

Plugged Waves is designed with the intentions of making a supplier to consumer relationship harmonious. Plugged Waves can act as storefront indie artist who is just trying to break into the music world. By giving independent artists another way of connecting with their supporters.
